Chemistry Science
What does the Law of Conservation of Mass state?
Difficulty: Easy Hover to reveal

Answer

The Law of Conservation of Mass states that matter is neither created nor destroyed during a chemical reaction. The mass of reactants is always equal to the mass of products.
